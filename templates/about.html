About Journal Ranker

Our vision is to build a seamless journal ranking system covering popular indexes across several field of study to enable a researcher publish his/her article in a journal thta would get more traction and citation count with less time and money spent.

What are the ranking journal features?
  • Subject Area of interest
  • Index of journals
  • Publisher of the journals
  • Citescore Percentile
  • Frequency of Publication
  • Open Access
  • Publication Duration
How do I rank the journals?

The user interface for the ranking is implemented with drag and drop functionlities. However, you must ensure that all the emelents of a feature are properly ranked before proceeding

What technique is used to implement ranking?

A Multi-Criteria Decision Making technique is applied to the ranking normaized input result and is used to make recommendation on the journal features.

Journal Ranker helps researcher to make decision in selecting the most suitable journal to publish their research work based on user preference.
